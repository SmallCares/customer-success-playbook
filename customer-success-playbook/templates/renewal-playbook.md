
# Renewal Playbook Template

## 1. Purpose
Consistent process for preparing, managing, and closing renewals.

## 2. Timeline
- 120–180 Days: contacts, usage fit, value review, risks  
- 90 Days: renewal pre‑brief, align with Sales/Finance  
- 60 Days: socialise terms, address blockers  
- 30 Days: final negotiation, approvals  
- 0 Days: signature, celebrate, update systems

## 3. Risk Assessment
| Factor | Good | Concern | Red Flag |
|--------|------|---------|---------|
| Adoption | High usage | Patchy | Declining |
| Executive Alignment | Strong | Change | None |

## 4. Prep Checklist
- Update Success Plan, ROI summary  
- Usage insights for Sales  
- Validate next‑term goals

## 5. Renewal Meeting Agenda
1) Objectives  2) Recap  3) Value  4) Adoption  5) Next‑term priorities  6) Proposal  7) Next steps

## 6. Proposal Components
Pricing, term options, volume changes, multi‑year incentives, add‑ons, services.

## 7. Escalation Path
CSM → CS Leader → Execs (as needed) + Legal/Finance/Product if required.

## 8. Close Checklist
Signed doc, CRM updated, forecast updated, handoffs, confirmation email.

## 9. Post‑Renewal Review
Lessons learned, competitive signals, process tweaks.
